About Me

I help brands turn complex retail data into clear, actionable stories.

I'm a Sales Insights Manager at Harvest Group, partnering with Kroger-focused CPG brands to uncover what truly drives their business—shopper behavior, category dynamics, digital performance, and emerging retail trends. At my best, I'm combining storytelling, data fluency, and creativity to help teams see what really matters, make smarter decisions, and move with confidence.
